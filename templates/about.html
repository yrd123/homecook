What is HomeCook?


Homefood is a platform which connects Vendors and Customers. Our mission is to provide a platform to all those who wants to sell food as well as make it easy for people like hostelites, employees, workers to find good quality food. The cutomers can order food from the vendor of their choice! Anyone willing to sell the food can sign up to our website and can sell the food of their choice. So, in this way, we provide job opportunities for all the food sellers out there and gives all the customers, the taste of home made food!
